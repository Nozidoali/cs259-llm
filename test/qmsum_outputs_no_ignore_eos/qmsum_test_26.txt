So the key points are:

- Trendwatchers surveyed cities in Paris and Milan about trends in clothing, shoes, and products
- Young and trendy people were more interested in fruit and vegetables than older people
- Material like wood was more popular among young people than older people
- Remote controls were more popular among young people than old people

Marketing also discussed the influence of materials like wood on remote controls, and that trends may change next year
